# Point Clear — Core Game Pillars

**[APPROVED FACT]**

These pillars are the standard every design proposal, task, and implementation decision is measured against. See also: [DESIGN_DNA.md](DESIGN_DNA.md), [PROJECT_BIBLE.md](PROJECT_BIBLE.md), [VISION.md](VISION.md).

1. **The game must be enjoyable when played solo.**
2. **Co-op should make the experience significantly better.**
3. **Build creation and experimentation are central to replayability.**
4. **Combat should be easy to understand but difficult to master.**
5. **Players should always have meaningful goals to pursue.**
6. **Seasonal competition should motivate players without making ordinary play feel pointless.**
7. **Leaderboards must represent fair and verifiable gameplay.**
8. **Systems must support future expansion without unnecessary rewrites.**
9. **Gameplay quality is more important than feature quantity.**
10. **Point Clear must maintain its own identity.**

## How to Use These Pillars

- Any new feature proposal should be checked against these pillars before it reaches Game Director approval.
- If a proposed system conflicts with a pillar, that conflict must be raised explicitly rather than resolved silently.
- These pillars are approved facts; they are not expected to change casually. Any change to this list is a decision in its own right and should be recorded in [DECISIONS.md](DECISIONS.md).
